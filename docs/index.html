JOAT is a multidisciplinary urban art festival and an international street dance event established in January 2014, in Montreal, Canada.

We are

Influencers who created an international interest in result to the vision of our brand and concept for our dance event. JOAT was produced in Taiwan, Singapore, India, UK, USA, Canada and soon in Japan. With an eager growing demand from other countries

JOAT evolved to a multidisciplinary street art festival in 2016, while keeping street dance as a primary focus.

MISSION

Jack Of All Trades core mission is to promote, support and celebrate practices stemming from urban / street / Hip Hop art forms while having dances stemming from Illusionary, Mechanical, Popping related and Boogaloo culture as anchoring narrative. Our manifestations are felt through festivals, events, education and creative activities while presenting these projects in a local, national and international scale. Creating a mixology in a ecosystem of urban / street / Hip Hop art forms and Popping/Boogaloo lineage and related cultures.

VISION

Support the development of urban / street / Hip Hop art forms and Illusionary, Mechanical, Popping/Boogaloo lineage and related dance cultures. To be a platform that adheres to its artistic, cultural, folk and community enrichment from it’s traditionalist stand points to the possible avant-garde ways.

VALUES

  • CULTURE: Promote, celebrate, honour and preserve art stemming from urban / street /Hip Hop culture and Illusionary, Mechanical, Popping/Boogaloo lineage and related dance cultures
  • MULTIDISCIPLINARY: A festival welcoming the different artistic expressions of those cultures and sub cultures; welcoming purist and fusionist.
  • CREATIVITY: Grant new creative ideas, support new and innovative art in the urban / street / Hip Hop culture.
  • EXCELLENCE: Encourage excellence and refinement, putting in the forefront our great professionals
  • EQUALITY: Inspire and put forward the ideology of an egalitarian artistic society in Quebec without any preference
  • DIVERSITY: Support artistic equality in Quebec by giving a share of attention and opportunities to communities of the “diversity'“ of Quebec and elsewhere
  • COMMUNITY: Inspire and educate the next actors of Popping/Boogaloo lineage and related cultures urban & inspire as well urban / street art / Hip Hop / culture, touching young artists and emerging professional artists
  • AUTHENTICITY: Assure and protect true and fair experiences of urban arts / street art / Hip Hop & Popping/Boogaloo lineage and related cultures
